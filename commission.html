Commissioned Art Pieces

This page showcases a timeline of my commissioned art projects, illustrating the growth of my skills and techniques over time. Each piece represents a milestone in my professional development, reflecting how my approach to meeting clients' visions and delivering customized artwork has evolved.

2024 | 2023 | 2021 | 2018 | 2017

2024

Gorillaz-All Here

“Gorillaz-All Here” was created on July 1, 2024, using a combination of fineliner, gel pens, color pencil, permanent markers, water markers, alcohol markers, and highlighter. This drawing features the popular UK artist Gorillaz, and its quality lies in the exactness of the details compared to the official poster. The combination of multiple materials makes this piece truly one of a kind, showcasing a unique blend of textures and colors that bring the artwork to life. I’m particularly proud of how the different mediums came together to create a cohesive and striking piece that captures the essence of the original poster.

Black Noir 5/19 Variant

“Black Noir 5/19 Variant” was crafted on June 4, 2024, using a blend of pencil, colored pencils, and permanent markers. This artwork serves as a custom comic cover for Black Noir from The Boys, showcasing a unique variation of the superhero’s suit. The meticulous planning, especially the anatomical precision of Black Noir, posed a significant challenge, yet it was accomplished with exquisite detail over time. The use of shading to depict a vivid, menacing storm in the backdrop, coupled with intricate elements like the barcode and sticker tag, rendering this piece exceptional.

2023

Master Roshi

“Master Roshi” was created on August 31, 2023, using a combination of fineliner, gel pens, color pencil, permanent markers, water markers, alcohol markers, and highlighter. This drawing features Master Roshi from Dragon Ball, adorned with graphic tattoos and smoking a cigarette. The heavy styling was referenced from an image online, but the size of the drawing made maintaining consistent lines and marker strokes a crucial element. The neon highlights on the glasses against the contrasting bright red creates a mesmerizing effect under color-changing lights, adding a dynamic and captivating touch to the piece.

Brook One Piece

“Brook One Piece” was created on February 9, 2023, using a combination of fineliner, gel pens, color pencil, permanent markers, water markers, alcohol markers, and highlighter. One of the main reasons this piece stands out to me is because I was able to maintain a professional and consistent level with the mix of materials, successfully executing what I had envisioned. Looking afar, this piece comes together with each individual aspect complimenting eachother. The consistency between the marker strokes in areas such as the hair, to the clothes was done to ensure a highly-rated, quality product.

2022

Wanted: Gear 5 Luffy

“Wanted: Gear 5 Luffy” was created on November 18, 2022, using fineliner and gel pens, color pencil, permanent markers, water markers, and alcohol markers. This drawing is a wanted poster of Monkey D. Luffy in his new Gear 5 form, inspired by the wanted posters that appear in the One Piece series. The piece captures the essence of the character and the iconic style of the series’ wanted posters, and I'm beyond satisfied with the quality of this drawing, as it came out perfectly, with precise lines, vibrant colors, and a professional finish that truly brings the character justice.

2021

Oni Samurai

“Oni Samurai” created on March 20, 2021, is a digital piece created using the Apple iPad, Apple Pencil, and the drawing software “Autodesk Sketchbook". It was a commissioned piece, meticulously drafted, created, and sold to a buyer. The design captures the fierce and imposing presence of the oni, blending traditional Japanese elements with a modern digital art style. The vibrant colors and detailed textures emphasize the character’s menacing yet captivating appearance. This piece showcases my ability to bring a client’s vision to life while maintaining a high level of artistic quality and creativity.

2018

Neon Skull

“Neon Skull” was created on October 19, 2018, using a combination of gel pen, fineliner, highlighter, and acrylic paint. The use of highlighter was particularly effective, as it exhibits a slight glow effect under neon or color-changing lights. This piece allowed me to experiment with different materials and techniques, pushing the boundaries of my expression and resulting in a piece that stands out for its bold effect.

Goku Ultra Instinct

“Goku Ultra Instinct” was created on March 22, 2018, using gel pen, fineliner, and color pencil. This drawing, made on a smaller piece of paper, features Goku in his Ultra Instinct form from Dragon Ball, with a small cricket tagging along the ride. I focused on creating a powerful yet consistent blend for the drawing, ensuring that the dynamic energy and intensity of the character were captured effectively. The careful blending of colors highlight the strength and aura of Goku in this form, and was commissioned as a friend's birthday gift.

Hatched Woamn

“Hatched Woman” was created on February 8, 2018, using a mix of ballpoint, fineliner, gel pens, and watercolor paints. This piece features a textured background with limited color to emphasize the cross-hatched lines. The intricate cross-hatching technique highlights the detailed features and expressions, bringing the subject to life. The blending of cool colors in the background creates a calm and serene environment, enhancing the overall impact of the piece.

2017

Astral Plane

“Astral Plane” was created on December 13, 2017, using a mix of fineliner pen, pencil, and watercolor paints. In this piece, I brought to life a vibrant and expressive galaxy with a multitude of colors and dynamic brushstrokes. The swirling patterns and vivid hues evoke a sense of wonder and exploration. I paid close attention to the interplay of cool and warm colors to create the vibrancy of outer space. This piece originally didn't' start out as a commission, rather it was sold after completion. This artwork is a testament to my creativity and ability to blend different mediums to produce a vibrant, one of a kind scene.

Gogeta Super Sayain 3

“Gogeta Super Saiyan 3” was created on August 25, 2017, using a mix of acrylics, pencil, and watercolor paints. This canvas painting features Vegeta from Dragon Ball in his Super Saiyan Blue form against a contrasting orange background. It was the first time I used both acrylic and watercolor paint on canvas, and I’m particularly proud of this piece, as it allowed me to explore new techniques and mediums, resulting in a vibrant and dynamic portrayal of the character.

Realstic Pug

“Realistic Pug” was created on May 23,2017, using only pencil. By sticking to one medium, it resulted in in a highly accurate drawing of a pug. This piece was my first commission, and I’m particularly proud of the level of detail and realism achieved using a day-to-day object. The meticulous attention to the the expressive eyes and the overall lifelike quality.
